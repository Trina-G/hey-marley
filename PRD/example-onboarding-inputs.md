## Notes for Testing

### Expected Classifications:

**Alex (Persona 1):** Intermediate
- Good ideas and details
- Some organization but could be stronger
- Decent mechanics with minor issues

**Jordan (Persona 2):** Beginner
- Very short responses
- Basic sentence structure
- Grammar and punctuation issues
- Limited detail

**Maya (Persona 3):** Advanced
- Rich details and vivid language
- Strong organization and flow
- Complex sentences
- Good mechanics

**Sam (Persona 4):** Beginner
- Minimal responses
- Very basic sentences
- Little detail or development
- Skipped optional question

**Chris (Persona 5):** Advanced
- Detailed and engaging
- Clear voice and personality
- Good structure and flow
- Strong vocabulary

**Priya (Persona 6):** Intermediate
- Good ideas and some vivid details
- Decent organization but could be tighter
- Mostly good mechanics
- Conversational voice

### Use Cases:

- **Persona 1 (Alex):** Typical student, shows the average case
- **Persona 2 (Jordan):** Tests minimum input handling, beginner classification
- **Persona 3 (Maya):** Tests advanced classification, rich content
- **Persona 4 (Sam):** Tests edge case - very short responses, skipped question
- **Persona 5 (Chris):** Tests passionate writer with organization challenges
- **Persona 6 (Priya):** Tests intermediate with good ideas but messy organization
